The week
Weekly principles
  • Every strength session opens with a warm-up: 10-min easy run OR plyo + pogos.
  • Block C is always optional — skip if recovery is poor or session running long, but do it most weeks.
  • Last set of every exercise: 1–2 reps shy of failure. If you could do 4 more, go heavier.
  • Add weight any session you hit the top of the rep range with clean form.
  • Skip the morning jog on Wednesday and Friday (leg days — keep the legs fresh).
Fat loss levers
  • The deficit creates fat loss. Training preserves muscle inside that deficit.
  • Protein target: 120–135g daily. Non-negotiable for muscle retention.
  • Track waist + hip weekly — the scale lies in the short term, especially with strength training.
  • If lifts drop after week 2, the deficit is too steep. Eat back 200 kcal.
